## 📊 How to Use This Dashboard

This dashboard helps you explore Netflix performance from different perspectives.

---

## 🔍 Two Ways to Explore

**1. Show-focused view**
- Analyze a specific show
- Understand how popular it is over time
- See how long it stays in the Top 10

**2. Country-focused view**
- Explore trends within a specific country
- Identify which shows are most popular
- Compare viewing patterns across regions
